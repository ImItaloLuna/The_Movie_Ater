Porco Rosso: The Last Sortie

紅の豚

Kurenai no Buta

Synopsis

In Italy, during the period between the two wars, with a background of economic recession and the rise of fascism, lost on a desert island in the Adriatic Sea, a former pilot emeritus of the Italian Air Force, finds himself transformed into a pig and becomes a become a bounty hunter. It's called "Porco Rosso". Aboard his red seaplane, he has many adventures: hunting down air pirates who have made a habit of stealing from tourists visiting the region, rescuing passengers taken hostage, aerial duels, seaplane "races", subterfuges to seed the Italian secret police... the pilot's life is hectic and full of adventures. Despite the apparent lightness of the film, which was originally designed to relax the Studio Ghibli teams, the anime Omohide Poro Poro had ended. Porco Rosso is an indictment against war and its absurdities, addressing themes such as the myth of the hero.
